Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 11, 2025

1. Information We Collect

During the verification process, we collect:

  • Your Discord User ID
  • Authentication codes provided by Discord OAuth2
  • Verification status and timestamps
  • Your IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, screen resolution, and pages visited

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Verifying your identity on our Discord server
  • Granting appropriate roles and permissions
  • Maintaining security and preventing abuse
  • Ensuring valid anti-raid traffic monitoring
  • Improving our verification system

3. Data Storage and Security

We store minimal data necessary for verification:

  • Discord User IDs are stored to maintain verification status
  • Authentication tokens are temporarily cached and not stored permanently
  • Data is stored on secure channels with limited access
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information

4. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell or rent your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties except as required by law
  • Use your information for marketing purposes

5. Discord Integration

Our service integrates with Discord's OAuth2 system. By using our verification service, you agree to:

  • Discord's Terms of Service
  • Discord's Privacy Policy
  • The limited data access required for verification

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your verification data
  • Opt-out of verification at any time
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or data removal requests, please contact us through our Discord server.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Continued use of our verification service constitutes acceptance of any changes.
